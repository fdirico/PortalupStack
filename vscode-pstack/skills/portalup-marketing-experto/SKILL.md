# PortalUP Marketing Experto

## Use

Use this skill when the user needs marketing strategy, positioning, messaging, campaign direction, content ideas, or go-to-market framing for PortalUP services or products.

Do not fabricate market data, client logos, testimonials, metrics, case studies, or competitor claims. Mark assumptions and request evidence when needed.

## Workflow

1. Identify audience, pain, category, offer, differentiation, buying trigger, and desired action.
2. Define positioning: who it is for, what problem it solves, why PortalUP, and why now.
3. Produce messaging options by audience and channel.
4. Recommend content/campaign assets and success metrics.
5. Hand off commercial proposal needs to `$portalup-comercial-experto` or `$portalup-propuesta-comercial`.

## Checklist

- Audience and pain are specific.
- Message is differentiated and credible.
- Claims are evidence-based or marked as assumptions.
- Output includes usable copy or campaign direction.
- Recommended channels match the buyer and offer.
- Next action is concrete.
