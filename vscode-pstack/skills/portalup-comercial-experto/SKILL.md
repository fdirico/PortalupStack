# PortalUP Comercial Experto

## Use

Use this skill when the user needs commercial judgment before writing a proposal, responding to a client, negotiating scope, or deciding whether an opportunity is worth pursuing.

Do not invent exact prices, legal terms, SLAs, discounts, margins, dates, or guarantees. Use ranges, options, assumptions, or placeholders when data is missing.

## Workflow

1. Identify client pain, urgency, decision maker, budget signal, success criteria, and current alternative.
2. Qualify opportunity: value, fit, risk, delivery feasibility, and strategic relevance.
3. Define commercial strategy: positioning, scope boundaries, options, pricing model, assumptions, and negotiation posture.
4. Prepare next action: discovery questions, proposal outline, client message, or internal go/no-go recommendation.
5. Hand off proposal drafting to `$portalup-propuesta-comercial` when the strategy is clear.

## Checklist

- Client pain is concrete, not generic.
- Scope and out-of-scope are protected.
- Value is tied to business outcome.
- Commercial options do not invent unsupported prices.
- Risks, dependencies, and assumptions are visible.
- Next step moves the deal forward.
