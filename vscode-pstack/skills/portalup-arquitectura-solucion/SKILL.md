# PortalUP Arquitectura de Solucion

## Use

Use this skill to turn requirements into a technical solution architecture or to review an existing architecture before proposal, delivery, or implementation.

Do not use it to invent missing platform versions, pricing, licensing, or client constraints. Mark unknowns and assumptions.

## Workflow

1. Capture business goal, current state, target users, systems, constraints, and non-functional requirements.
2. Define components, integrations, data flows, security, operations, deployment, monitoring, and support model.
3. Identify risks, dependencies, assumptions, alternatives, and phased delivery plan.
4. Produce a decision-ready architecture summary with open questions.

```text
- ...

- ...

- ...

- ...
```

## Checklist

- Business goal and technical constraints are explicit.
- Components and ownership are named.
- Data flow, authentication, authorization, and auditability are covered.
- Operations include monitoring, backup, rollback, and support.
- Risks, dependencies, and unknowns are visible.
- Proposal language stays realistic and implementation-ready.
