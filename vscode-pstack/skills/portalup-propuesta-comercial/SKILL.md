# PortalUP Propuesta Comercial

## Use

Use this skill to transform client needs into a clear commercial proposal.

Do not invent prices, dates, or team allocation if the user did not provide them. Use placeholders or options.

## Workflow

1. Identify client pain, desired outcome, constraints, and urgency.
2. Define scope, out of scope, assumptions, deliverables, phases, team, risks, and commercial model.
3. Produce a client-ready draft with optional pricing placeholders.

## Output

```text
Resumen ejecutivo

Dolor del cliente

Objetivo de la propuesta

Alcance

Fuera de alcance

Supuestos

Entregables

Cronograma

Equipo sugerido

Riesgos y mitigaciones

Modelo comercial
- Valor unico:
- Valor mensual:
- Opciones:

Siguiente paso
```

## Checklist

- Separate scope from out of scope.
- Make assumptions visible.
- Include business value, not only tasks.
- Include risks and dependencies.
- Keep language professional and concrete.
