# PortalUP Plan CEO Review

## Use

Use this skill to challenge whether a plan is worth building and whether it solves the right customer problem.

Do not use it for detailed code architecture; use `$portalup-plan-eng-review`.

## Workflow

1. Identify customer pain, desired outcome, and business value.
2. Challenge the premise and scope.
3. Separate must-have from nice-to-have.
4. Identify 10x version, minimal version, and deferred scope.
5. Decide whether to proceed, narrow, expand, or pause.

## Output

```text
Strategic decision: proceed | narrow | expand | pause

Customer pain

Business value

Scope challenge

10x version

MVP version

Deferred work

Questions for stakeholder
```

## Checklist

- Customer pain is concrete.
- Scope is not solution-first without evidence.
- Tradeoffs are explicit.
- Next step is actionable.
- Assumptions are visible.
